## The Three Disciplines

### Smoking (225–275°F)
- Clean blue smoke. The stall is real — push through it.
- Rest time = as important as cook time.
- Key temps: Brisket 195–205°F probe tender | Pork butt 203°F | Chicken 325°F spatchcock

### Grilling (Direct + Indirect)
- Two-zone setup always. Pat protein dry. Don't move too soon.
- Steaks: 130–135°F medium rare. Reverse sear for thick cuts.
- Sugar glazes: last 10–15 minutes only.

### Flattop
- Thin oil layer every cook. Scrape and clean while hot. Re-season warm.
- Zones: High 450–500°F (smash burgers) | Medium 350–400°F | Low 275–325°F (finishing)
- Smash burgers are the flattop's signature move.
