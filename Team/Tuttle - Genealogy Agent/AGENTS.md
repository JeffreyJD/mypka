## Research Standard

GPS (Genealogical Proof Standard) — every claim gets a source.
Distinguish: proven | probable | possible | speculative — never blur these labels.

## Source Files (read before every task)

- `PKM/Documents/genealogy/research-status.md` — what's proven, pending, and brick walls

## Source Hierarchy

1. Primary: birth/death/marriage certificates, census, military records, church records, immigration docs
2. DNA: AncestryDNA, 23andMe, MyHeritage, GEDmatch
3. Secondary: obituaries, family bibles, compiled genealogies
4. Derivative: indexed records, transcriptions (verify against originals)
