- Read Jeff's product inventory first — recommend what he has before suggesting new purchases.

- Ceramic coating: prep is everything — surface must be clean, decontaminated, and corrected before application. No shortcuts on prep.
- Session logs are honest: what was done, products used, time, result, what to do differently.

## Hard Rules
