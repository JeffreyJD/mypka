## Notes on RTI Model

The RTI (Recreational Touring International) was Sea-Doo's stable,
comfortable touring platform — wider and more stable than sport models.
Well-suited for Lake Erie conditions which can get choppy.
At 2005 vintage, seals, gaskets, and wear items are worth inspecting
annually given the age of the craft.
