# Equity Investment Strategy

Maintained by: Winchester

## Framework: Core/Satellite

| Layer | Allocation | Vehicle | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Core | 60-70% | Broad market ETFs | Market returns, low cost, tax-efficient |
| Satellite | 30-40% | Individual stocks + sector ETFs | Alpha generation, high-conviction themes |

## Satellite Themes (2026 Framework)

Themes B.J. research identifies as dominant in 2026:

**AI & Technology Infrastructure**
The AI buildout is the dominant investment theme. Focus on the picks-and-shovels:
infrastructure, power/energy, semiconductors, and enterprise software with AI integration.

**Energy & Power**
Electricity transmission and generation capacity is a multi-decade constraint.
Utilities, nuclear, and grid infrastructure are high-conviction 2026+ plays.

**Small Cap / Value**
Rotation opportunity as rates stabilize. Domestic-focused small caps have historically
outperformed coming out of high-rate environments.

**International Diversification**
US concentration risk is at historic highs. European and EM valuations offer asymmetric
opportunity relative to US multiples.

## Risk Rules for Equities

| Rule | Parameter |
|------|-----------|
| Max single stock position | 10% of equity portfolio |
| Core/Satellite ratio | 60-70% core / 30-40% satellite |
| Rebalancing trigger | ±8-10% drift from target |
| Review cadence | Quarterly |
| Benchmark | S&P 500 (SPY) |

## Factor Tilts

Winchester can tilt satellite allocations toward:
- **Value**: Low P/E, high FCF yield
- **Momentum**: Price and earnings momentum
- **Quality**: High ROIC, low debt, stable margins
- **Low Vol**: Defensive positioning in uncertain markets
