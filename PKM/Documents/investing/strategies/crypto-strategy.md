# Crypto Investment Strategy — Core/Explore Model

Maintained by: Winchester
Last updated: 2026-04-13

## The Model

Jeffrey's crypto strategy uses a **Core/Explore** framework:

| Layer | Allocation | Assets | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|--------|---------|
| Core | 60% | BTC, ETH | Store of value + yield infrastructure |
| Explore | 40% | XRP, ADA, HBAR, XLM | High-beta utility/payments layer |

## Core Layer — BTC + ETH

### Bitcoin (BTC)
- Role: Digital gold. Macro hedge. Institutional-grade store of value.
- Strategy: Long-term hold. Do not trade the core.
- DCA: Structured buys on significant drawdowns (>20% from ATH)
- Target allocation: TBD % of crypto portfolio

### Ethereum (ETH)
- Role: Yield-bearing infrastructure. The settlement layer for DeFi and tokenization.
- Strategy: Hold + stake where possible for yield. Track staking APY as income.
- Target allocation: TBD % of crypto portfolio

## Explore Layer — XRP, ADA, HBAR, XLM

Higher beta, higher conviction required. Each capped at 10% of total crypto portfolio individually.

| Asset | Thesis | Risk Level |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| XRP | Cross-border payments, Ripple institutional adoption, post-SEC clarity | Medium-High |
| ADA | Smart contract platform, academic rigor, developing world focus | High |
| HBAR | Hashgraph consensus, enterprise adoption, HBAR Foundation | High |
| XLM | Stellar payments network, IBM partnership, remittances | Medium-High |

## Risk Rules for Crypto

| Rule | Parameter |
|------|-----------|
| Max single Explore position | 10% of crypto portfolio |
| Rebalancing trigger | ±8-10% drift from target weight |
| Stablecoin dry powder | 5-10% of crypto portfolio |
| DCA on drawdowns | Deploy reserves when BTC drops >20% from recent ATH |
| Review cadence | Quarterly minimum; ad hoc on major market events |

## On-Chain Signals Winchester Monitors

- BTC exchange reserves (declining = bullish supply squeeze)
- ETH staking ratio (higher staking = less sell pressure)
- Stablecoin supply trends (growth = capital ready to deploy)
- Miner/validator outflows
- Fear & Greed Index (extremes signal reversal opportunities)

## Tax Notes

- Track cost basis per lot (FIFO default unless Jeffrey specifies specific lot)
- LT capital gains: hold >12 months where possible
- Tax-loss harvesting: evaluate Explore positions in down years
- Staking rewards: taxable as income at time of receipt (consult CPA)
